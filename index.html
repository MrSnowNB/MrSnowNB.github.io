Mark Snow


I am an educator and life-long learner. I am currently working on learning how to code. While I have been teaching high school engineering for 10 years now, most of my work has been in a traditional materials lab. I do have a few years experience in teaching Vex Robotics Programming at the High School Level. Recently my interest has turned to coding. I am working with my students using freeCodeCamps and we are all learning togather. This page serves as a learning and teaching tool for myself and my students. I hope to continue this journey outside of the classroom and to keep expanding my horizons.
